ABOUT SKY SECURITY

Sky Security & Training is a professional training institution based in Malamulele, Limpopo. We specialize in PSIRA grades, firearms, and advanced security courses for individuals and companies.

With years of experience and qualified instructors, we are dedicated to providing quality training that meets industry standards. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every graduate is prepared for real-world security challenges.
